Our Story

We are Kai, Danny & Josie, co-founders of Bring On Monday and united by our love and passion for using business as a force for good.

Between us we have built successful companies (Kai & Danny Co-founded Duffle&Co), worked for large corporations, lectured in business and had 20+ years of real life business experience. This includes the ups, the downs and everything in between. From this, we know two things for certain:

1) A lending hand from pros can propel small businesses forward, fast.

2) Numbers are everything but accountants and financial support are pricey.

It’s difficult to imagine where we’d be now if we had asked for the support that we actually needed back then. However, as young millennials we chose to be stubborn. We did everything ourselves, chose the hardest and longest paths possible, and took on too much. But you know what? We are glad that we did! Being in those trenches gave us the opportunity to learn the lessons that bring you value, and form this frickin epic company called Bring On Monday!

There’s no doubt in our minds that good financial practices are critical for business success. Yet, accountants are hella expensive. Maybe you're happy to pay for one because you can thank your lucky stars that he or she cares for your cause, but do they share your entrepreneurial mindset? Would your accountant go the extra mile to help you in any way possible? Would they coach you through external factors such as importing products, connect you with their network or send a financial report suggesting how you could increase profit?

Many founders want to ‘go by the book’, but are not 100% sure that what they are doing is correct. This is not only stressful but also time consuming! What’s clear is that most business owners want to spend their time (and money) on their passion; they don’t want to deal with paying taxes, chasing up receipts and reconciling their accounts.

So, financial administration, accounting and boredom relief aside, Bring On Monday exists to help your business in any way we can to help GROW your business. We are here to empower those businesses that will shape a better tomorrow by providing innovative accounting & administration, insightful reporting and professional coaching, at revolutionary prices.
